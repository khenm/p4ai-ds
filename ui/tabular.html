Proportional distribution of adoption speeds by health status.

Vaccination status vs adoption speed proportional breakdown.

Key Findings

  • Healthy pets dominate the dataset and show a moderately balanced adoption distribution
  • Vaccinated pets tend to have slightly faster adoption compared to unvaccinated ones
  • Very few pets have serious injuries — sample sizes too small for strong conclusions

Key Insights

Dataset Characteristics

  • Class imbalance exists — Speed 4 (no adoption) is the largest class
  • Strong correlations between Vaccinated, Dewormed, and Sterilized
  • Minimal missing data — Description has highest missing rate
  • Fee has a wide range with many free listings

Recommendations

  • Apply SMOTE or class weights for imbalanced adoption speeds
  • Encode State as categorical or extract top-N states
  • Consider combining Vaccinated/Dewormed/Sterilized into a health composite
  • Feature engineering: interaction terms between Age and Fee
